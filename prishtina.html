Welcome to Prishtina

Prishtina, the vibrant capital of Kosovo, is a city rich in history and culture. From its bustling markets to its historical landmarks, Prishtina offers a unique blend of old and new. Explore the city's lively cafes, visit the stunning National Library, and take a stroll through Germia Park.

Tourist Information

Whether you're here for a short stay or an extended visit, Prishtina has something for everyone. Don't miss the chance to experience the local cuisine, visit the Ethnological Museum, and enjoy the nightlife. The city is also a gateway to the rest of Kosovo, with easy access to beautiful landscapes and historic sites.

  • Best time to visit: Spring and Autumn
  • Local attractions: Newborn Monument, Skanderbeg Square
  • Getting around: The city is well connected with buses and taxis. Walking is also a great way to explore.
  • Accommodation: A range of hotels and hostels to suit all budgets
